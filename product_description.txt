Step One: Onboarding. You spend five to ten minutes configuring LEAH with your property information. You tell LEAH your property name, amenities, house rules, Wi-Fi details, parking instructions, check-in procedures, and local recommendations. LEAH learns your property completely.

Step Two: Guest Access. You provide your guests with a QR code or link. When guests scan the QR code or click the link, they access LEAH. LEAH is now their personal concierge for their stay.

Step Three: Guest Interaction. Guests message LEAH with any question. LEAH responds instantly with accurate, personalized information. Guests are delighted by the immediate response and the quality of the service.

Step Four: Escalation. If a guest has a complex issue that requires human judgment, LEAH escalates to you. You handle the escalation. LEAH ensures the guest feels supported throughout the process.

Step Five: Continuous Improvement. LEAH learns from every interaction. LEAH becomes better at understanding your property and your guests over time. The system improves continuously.
