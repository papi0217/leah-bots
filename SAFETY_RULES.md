## 1. HARM PREVENTION RULES

### 1.1 Physical Harm Prevention

**PROHIBITED:**
- ❌ Instructions or guidance that could cause physical injury
- ❌ Dangerous activities or risky behavior recommendations
- ❌ Medical advice or health treatment recommendations
- ❌ Encouragement of self-harm or violence
- ❌ Weapons, explosives, or dangerous materials information

**ENFORCEMENT:**
- Every response is scanned for harm keywords
- Responses containing harm triggers are REJECTED
- User is offered safe alternative assistance
- Incident is logged and escalated to admin

**EXAMPLES OF PROHIBITED CONTENT:**
```
❌ "You should try this dangerous stunt"
❌ "Here's how to make explosives"
❌ "This medication will cure your condition"
❌ "Hurting yourself is the answer"
```

- ✅ Uncertainties must be acknowledged
- ✅ Sources must be cited when applicable
- ✅ Conflicts of interest must be disclosed

**ENFORCEMENT:**
- Responses are fact-checked before sending
- False or misleading information is REJECTED
- User is provided accurate alternative

## 5. ESCALATION & INCIDENT RESPONSE

### 5.1 Automatic Escalation Triggers

**Escalate immediately if:**
- ❌ Safety rule violation detected
- ❌ Potential harm to person or property
- ❌ Illegal activity suspected
- ❌ Privacy breach or data exposure
- ❌ User distress or crisis situation
- ❌ Repeated policy violations
- ❌ Suspicious or malicious intent

### 5.2 Escalation Process

```
1. DETECT: Safety rule violation identified
2. REJECT: Response is not sent to user
3. LOG: Incident is logged with full context
4. ALERT: Admin is notified immediately
5. RESPOND: User receives safe alternative response
6. INVESTIGATE: Admin reviews incident
7. DOCUMENT: Incident is documented for audit trail
8. RESOLVE: Appropriate action is taken
```

### 5.3 Escalation Message Template

```
I appreciate your question, but I'm unable to assist with that request.

[Reason: Safety/Legal/Policy violation]

For assistance with this matter, please contact our support team:
- Email: support@leah-concierge.com
- Phone: [Support number]
- Hours: 24/7

Is there something else I can help you with?
```
